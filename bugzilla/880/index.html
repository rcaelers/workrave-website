Rob Caelers  Jan 20 2010 20:46:55 UTC
Workrave puts icons on the buttons of the exercise windows. However, Ubuntu (or Gnome, not sure) disabled these icons by default.

You can enable the icon by opening gconf-editor, navigate to '/desktop/gnome/interface' and check 'button_have_icons'.

I personally think a top level window should at least have some large buttons on the bottom. And even though I like icons the the buttons, overriding the gtk theme is not a good idea I think.
