Comment 1
Kees-Jan Dijkzeul  Jul 2 2004 07:43:20 UTC
This usually happens when you ignore a break. For example, if you are working,
the micropause timer counts down. When it reaches zero, you are asked to take a
break.  When you don't, the timer keeps on counting down (hence it gets negative).

Could you please open the preferences window, take a look a the "micro pause"
tab, and not the "postpone time" (it's the third timer value from the top). When
the timer ever gets negative, that's the time at which you should be told to
take a break. I.e. my postpone time is 2:30. So whenever the micropause timer
reaches a multiple of -2:30 (i.e. 2:30, 5:00, 7:30 and so on) I get told to take
a break.

So, if you, too, get warnings whenever you reach your (negative) postpone time,
then you're observing correct behaviour. Otherwise, this is indeed a bug. Could
you please double check?

Thanks!
