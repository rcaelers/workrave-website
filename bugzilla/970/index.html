Description
Walter  Jan 21 2011 10:48:33 UTC
Hi,

When I temporarily change the Mode from Normal to Suspended or Quiet and forget to turn it back at the end of the day, the next day after rebooting the PC Workrave still is in the very same mode as where it was left in.

This means that although it is auto-started you don't get any warning to take a micro or rest break. For me this means that I only detect it when you already feel the effects of working without pause for too long.

To prevent people from coming into that forgotten mode, a new day should mean that Workrave resets to normal mode by default: at least after booting, but maybe even after e.g. 3 hours of inactivity.
You could consider an opt-out from that standard mode reset, for people who want to use Workrave for just some monitoring functionality via quiet mode. But by default protect people as that is what Workrave is installed for!

I entered this as an enhancement request, as I don't know whether maybe an other OS/Hardware/Version *does* reset the mode after reboot. For now I assume that mode resetting is not meant to be happening.
If my assumption is wrong and on other OS/Hardware/Version mode resetting *is* working, then Severity -> Normal, Hardware -> PC, OS -> Windows 7.

Kind regards,
Walter.
