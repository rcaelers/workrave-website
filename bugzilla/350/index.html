Walter  Jan 20 2011 09:45:35 UTC
Hi,

Just started using Workrave this week, as my company stopped with Workpace. And I'm (just as MANY users) very satisfied with your splendid program!
The one big thing that I am missing in Workrave, I found it here in this Feature Request: timers.

But in addition to the proposed timers, which all correspond to breaks (that last for a certain user configurable time), I also would like to get a warning that the time is reached when I want to stop working (different per day). I already noticed that several days I continued working for much too long compared to what is healthy. As workaround I had to solve it in yet another program, while this is rather basic anti-RSI functionality hence could very well be part of Workrave. Maybe it is also good to raise prio?

So in short: I plea for not just a break indicator that lasts a certain time, but also for a working day indicator.

Regards,
Walter.
