Description
Kees-Jan Dijkzeul  Apr 1 2008 21:27:44 UTC
This is with daily build 20080327

I just had a daily limit (restbreak was way overdue), the break window appeared, I clicked postpone.

Now, WR has literally started a new day for me: I am allowed to do a full days work, and in the statistics window, data is updated for april 2nd, even though it is still april 1st (no joke, though). According to the statistics window, I worked today until 3.59 AM (even though it is not yet that late). On april 2nd, I have already worked from 4.04 am to 4:23 am (though I hope to be asleep by then.

Navigating the calendar is also weird. Example testcase:
- Select today (april 1st)
- Click "left arrow" three times.
- Click "right arrow" four times.

Expected result:
- Considering that there are statistics for april 2nd, I'd expect to end up at april 2nd.

Actual result:
- Any click beyond the third does not have any effect. We will not go to april 2nd.
- Workaround: leave the "right arrow" button and enter it again. Then click. We are now at april 2nd.

See also attached screenshots.
