First of I have to say this - Workrave is an awesome program! It keeps
me fresh and real!

I found a minor bug (I don't even know if I can really call it a bug).
Anyways. If I have a menu unfolded in any application in Windows and
Workrave pops up with micro brake counter, then when the counter reaches
1, the screen does not go away until I leave the menu.

I don't know if I explained it well, so here is a real life example. I
am in writing a document using MS Word. I am about to save it, so I
click menu "File" and about to choose "Save", but before I do that
Workrave pops up with 30 second break. It counts down to 1 second and
then just sits there. The window does not go away until I leave the
"File" menu in word, then Workrave instantly goes away.

My settings are not to warn about the breaks and it locks all input.
Though when I second is left, my input works fine - I am able to close
the menu.
