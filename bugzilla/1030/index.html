Rob Caelers  Oct 22 2011 19:03:54 UTC
The natural restbreak can be turned off in the preferences: Timers -> Restbreak --> Start nartual restbreak when screen is locked.

How does your personal computer take trak of you breaks if you are working on the proprietary computer? Did you turn on the reading mode?
