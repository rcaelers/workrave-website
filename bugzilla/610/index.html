Comment 1
Kees-Jan Dijkzeul  Mar 28 2007 08:13:39 UTC
(In reply to comment #0)

I'm seeing similar behavior on occasion. Does the window have an orange flashing bar? That would mean that workrave thinks you are still active. I'm not sure what causes it (workrave missing some mouse-up event or something), but clicking the break window a few times and/or tapping a few buttons (alt, shift and control sometimes seem to get stuck in the "down" state) usually resolves it for me.

Note: After finishing the clicking and key-pressing, you should wait for another five seconds before the orange bar will disappear and the counter will resume.
